A [[Vector Space]] has a unique additive identity.

##### Proof:
Let $V$ a vector space with additive identities $\mathbf{0}$ and $\mathbf{0}'$, then
$$
\begin{align}
	\mathbf{0} &= \mathbf{0}'+ \mathbf{0}\\
	&= \mathbf{0} + \mathbf{0}'\\
	&= \mathbf{0}'
\end{align}
$$
Therefore $\mathbf{0}$ is unique in $V$.
