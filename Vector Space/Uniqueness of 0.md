A [[Vector Space]] has a unique additive identity.

##### Proof:
Let $V$ a vector space with additive identities $ bold(0)$ and $ bold(0)'$, then
$$

	 bold(0) &=  bold(0)'+  bold(0)\
	&=  bold(0) +  bold(0)'\
	&=  bold(0)'

$$
Therefore $ bold(0)$ is unique in $V$.
