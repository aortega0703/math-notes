In a [[Vector Space]] $V$, $-(-v)=v$ for all $v\in V$.
##### Proof:
$$
\begin{align}
	- (-v) + (-v)
		&= \mathbf{0}\\
	- (-v) + (-v) + v &= v\\
	- (-v) + (-v + v) &= v\\
	-(-v) &= v
\end{align}
$$
