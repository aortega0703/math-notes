In a [[Vector Space]] $V$, $0v=\mathbf{0}$ for all $v\in V$. 
##### Proof:
$$
\begin{align}
	0v &= (0+0)v\\
		&= 0v + 0v\\
	0v - 0v &= 0v + 0v - 0v\\
	\mathbf{0} &= 0v
\end{align}
$$
