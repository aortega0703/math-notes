In a [[Vector Space]] $V$ over $F$, $a\mathbf{0}=\mathbf{0}$ for all $a\in F$.
##### Proof:
$$
\begin{align}
	a\mathbf{0} 
		&= a(\mathbf{0} + \mathbf{0})\\
		&= a\mathbf{0} + a\mathbf{0}\\
	a\mathbf{0} - a\mathbf{0} 
		&= a\mathbf{0} + a\mathbf{0} - a\mathbf{0}\\
	\mathbf{0} &= a\mathbf{0}
\end{align}
$$
