In a [[Vector Space]] $V$ over $F$, $a bold(0)= bold(0)$ for all $a in F$.
##### Proof:
$$

	a bold(0) 
		&= a( bold(0) +  bold(0))\
		&= a bold(0) + a bold(0)\
	a bold(0) - a bold(0) 
		&= a bold(0) + a bold(0) - a bold(0)\
	 bold(0) &= a bold(0)

$$
