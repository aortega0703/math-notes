For a [[Set]] $F$, the [[Set]] $F^oo$ of all sequences of elements of $F$ is defined as:$$
F^oo := {(x_1, x_2, dots);space x_k in F #[for] k in {1, 2, dots}}
$$
# Notation
- If $x$ is a sequence, then $x_i$ denotes the $i^#[th]$ component of $x$.
- $bold(0) := (0, dots)$
