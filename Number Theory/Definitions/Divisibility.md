For $s,t in ZZ$ with $t != 0$
- If $s=td$ for some $d in ZZ$, then $t$ _divides_ $s$ (denoted $t|s$) and $s$ is a _multiple_ of $t$.
- A _prime_ is an integer $p>1$ whose only positive divisors are $1$ and $p$ itself.
