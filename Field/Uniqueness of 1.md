A [[Field]] has a unique multiplicative identity.
##### Proof:
Let $F$ be a field with multiplicative identities $1$ and $1'$, then
$$
\begin{align}
	1 &= 1'1\\
	&= 1 1'\\
	&= 1'
\end{align}
$$
Therefore 1 is unique in $F$.
