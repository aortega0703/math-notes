In a [[Field]] $F$, $-a$ is unique for all $a\in F$.
##### Proof:
Let $a\in F$ with additive inverses $b$ and $c$, then
$$
\begin{align}
	b &= 0 + b\\
		&= (c + a) + b\\
		&= c + (a + b)\\
		&= c + 0\\
		&= c
\end{align}
$$
Therefore $-a$ is unique.
