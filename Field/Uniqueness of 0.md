A [[Field]] has a unique additive identity.
##### Proof:
Let $F$ be a field with additive identities $0$ and $0'$, then
$$
\begin{align}
	0 &= 0' + 0\\
	&= 0 + 0'\\
	&= 0
\end{align}
$$
Therefore $0$ is unique in $F$.
