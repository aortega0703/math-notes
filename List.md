For a [[Set]] $F$, the [[Set]] $F^n$ of all _lists_ of length $n in NN$ is defined as:$$
F^n := {(x_1, dots, x_n); space x_i in F #[for] i in {1, dots, n} }
$$
# Notation
- If $x$ is a list of length $n$, then $x_i$ denotes the $i^#[th]$ component of $x$.
- $bold(0) := (0, dots, 0)$
