- The [[Set]] of all integers is denoted by $ ZZ$[^1].
- The [[Set]] of all positive integers is denoted by $ ZZ^+$.
- The [[Set]] of all non-zero integers is denoted by $ ZZ^*$.
[^1]: The notation comes from the German _Zahlen_ (numbers).
