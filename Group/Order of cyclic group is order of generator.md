For a [[Group]] $G$ and $a\in G$, $|a| = |\langle a \rangle|$.
##### Proof:
By [[Equality of different exponents criterion]] $\langle a\rangle = \{a, a^2, \dots, a^{|a|}\}$ so $|a| = \langle a \rangle$
