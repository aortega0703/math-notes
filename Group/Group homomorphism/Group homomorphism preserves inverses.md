For any [[Group homomorphism]] $\phi:G\to H$$$
	\phi(g^{-1})=\phi(g)^{-1}
$$
##### Proof:
Let $g in G$, then by [[Group homomorphisms preverse identity]]$$

	e_H &= \phi(e_G)\
	&= \phi(gg^{-1})\
	&= \phi(g)\phi(g^{-1})\
	\phi(g)^{-1} &= \phi(g)^{-1}\phi(g)\phi(g^{-1})\
	&= \phi(g^{-1})

$$
