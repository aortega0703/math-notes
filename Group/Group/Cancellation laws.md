In a [[Group]] $G$, $ba = ca ==> b=c$ and $ab = ac ==> b=c$ for all $a,b,c in G$.
##### Proof:
- $ba = ca ==> b=c$:$$
	
		ba &= ca\
		baa^{-1} &= ca a^{-1}\
		be &= ce\
		b&=c
	$$
- $ab = ac ==> b=c$:$$
	
		ab &= ac\
		a^{-1} ab &= a^{-1} a c\
		eb &= ec\
		b &= c
	$$
