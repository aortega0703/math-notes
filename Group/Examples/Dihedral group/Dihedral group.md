For $(n\geq3)\in\mathbb{Z}$ the _Dihedral group_ $D_{2n}$ is the group generated by two elements $\sigma,\tau$ such that$$
	\sigma^n=1,\quad \tau^2=1,\quad \tau\sigma\tau=\sigma^{-1}
$$
