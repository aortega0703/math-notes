For $k in ZZ/n$
$$\langle k \rangle= ZZ/n <==> \operatorname{gcd}(n,k)=1$$
##### Proof:
$ ZZ/n = \langle 1 \rangle$ and $k=1^k$, the result then follows from [[Generators of Finite Cyclic Groups]].
