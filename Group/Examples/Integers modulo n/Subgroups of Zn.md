For each positive divisor $k$ of $n$, the set $\langle n/k \rangle$ is the unique subgroup of $ ZZ_n$ of order $k$; moreover, these are the only subgroups of $ ZZ_n$.
##### Proof:
Direct result of [[Fundamental Theorem of Cyclic Groups]].
