A _group isomorphism_ $\phi:G \to H$ is an [[Isomorphism]] in $\mathbf{Grp}$, that is, an [[Group homomorphism]] with an [[Isomorphism|Inverse]] $\phi^{-1}:H\to G$ that is also an homomorphism.
