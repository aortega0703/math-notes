For objects $A,B,X$, if there are arrows $f,g:A+ B\to X$ such that$$
\iota_1 f=\iota_1g, quad
\iota_2f=\iota_2g
$$then $f=g$ ($\iota_1$ and $\iota_2$ are said to be _jointly monic_).
##### Proof:
Dual of [[Product projections are jointly epic]].
