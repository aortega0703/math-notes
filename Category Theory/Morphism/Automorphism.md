An _automorphism_ is an [[Isomorphism]] $f$ with $\operatorname{Dom}(f) = \operatorname{Cod}(f)$. The collection of automorphisms of $A$ is denoted $\operatorname{Aut}(A)\subseteq \operatorname{End}(A)$.
