Every [[Category|Identity morphism]] $op("id")_A:A -> A$ is an [[Isomorphism]] with itself as its inverse.
##### Proof:
$op("id")_A op("id")_A = op("id")_A$, so it is its own left and right inverse, and therefore an isomorphism.
